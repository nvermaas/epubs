Rondane National Park, Noorwegen, 1998 - 4 dagen

  • website

    • 4 days into Rondane National Park with my brother. One of my early hikes, where I made a beginners mistake. I got totally soaked from walking through low bushes, despite it being a sunny day, despite the rain gear in my backpack. I got so wet, and so thoroughly cold, that we could not sit down to rest. Exhaustion and hypothermia became real risks, and then we reached heaven. Bjornhollia Hut!
