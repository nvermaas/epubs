    • The newly created 'East Highland Way' starts in Fort William, where the classic 'West Highland Way' ends. I took Nicole on her first long distant hike. We met druids, midges and hungry women and we had a splendid time. The EHW can't really compare with the WHW, but even so it was worth it.
